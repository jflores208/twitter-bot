MY TRAVELS AROUND THE WORLD

How it started...

Three years ago I started a job with a company I found on Instagram, and decided to drop everything and start a new journey. They told me to pack a bag and that I would be on the first flight out to Orlando, Florida. I had no idea what to expect ,but I got on that red eye flight not knowing my life would be changed forever.

When I flew out I had no idea what my job was going to be like. All I knew was that I was going to be selling some kind of "hot and cold packs", but soon enough I became one of the top sales people of Spartan Mindset.

The company flew me to a new city each week all expenses paid for.

I left my comfort zone to start a new life.
